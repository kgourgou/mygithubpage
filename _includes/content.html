About me

I am a fifth year Ph.D student at UMass Amherst, in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics. I am co-advised by Professor Markos Katsoulakis and Professor Luc Rey-Bellet.

My Research

Currently, I'm trying to learn more about systems in molecular dynamics and the schemes involved in their simulation.

The point of contact of the different projects I am working on is to employ tools from information theory in new ways to generate insights and quantify uncertainties.

Code

You can find all of my code on Github.

Data Science

Due to my interest in data science, I co-founded the Graduate Researchers interested in Data (GRiD) group. I served as Co-Chair of the group during the fall semester of 2014 and the spring semester of 2015. As a Co-Chair, I helped organize UMass Amherst HackEbola and the 2015 ASA DataFest. I also served as a Chief Technical Officer during the Fall 2015 and Spring 2016.

I am also an assistant organizer for the Western Mass Data Science, Stats, and R group.
